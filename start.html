Male Hair

The medium length hair is probably one of the most generic hairstyles in anime and manga, very common to the "average guy" protagonist.

Before drawing the hair, draw an outline of the head along with the hairline first.

Women

Before drawing the head, make a straight vertical line through the middle of where you wish to position it. This line acts as a guide to keep both sides equal in width and helps position the facial features later on.

Making Sushi

To draw a maki roll begin with its outer shape that should look like a short cylinder. Make sure the bottom part of each roll is slightly rounder than the top — otherwise the roll will look odd.
